INTRODUCTION					*ctrlp-J6uil-introduction*

|J6uil| extension for |CtrlP|.


==============================================================================
REQUIREMENTS					*ctrlp-J6uil-requirements*

- Vim 7.3 or later
- |CtrlP|
- |J6uil|

|CtrlP|		(http://ctrlpvim.github.com/ctrlp.vim)
|J6uil|		(https://github.com/basyura/J6uil.vim)


==============================================================================
INTERFACE					*ctrlp-J6uil-interface*
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMMANDS					*ctrlp-J6uil-commands*

:CtrlPJ6uilRooms					*:CtrlPJ6uilRooms*
	Browse J6uil rooms.
	Also see |<Plug>(J6uil_unite_rooms)|.

:CtrlPJ6uilMembers					*:CtrlPJ6uilMembers*
	Browse J6uil members of room.
	Also see |<Plug>(J6uil_unite_members)|.
